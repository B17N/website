About BlueStone

Our Company

BlueStone is a blockchain-based NFT platform specializing in tools that simplify NFT creation and facilitate innovation around NFT utility. We operate within the expanding markets of crypto event ticketing and NFT-based games, positioning ourselves as tech innovators in the rapidly evolving landscape of digital assets.

Our solutions cater to two primary sectors:

  • Event Ticketing: We offer NFT ticketing services for crypto events, providing full lifecycle management from ticket creation to redemption.
  • Gaming: We provide a blockchain-based NFT tooling suite for gaming companies, allowing them to issue game items and characters as NFT assets.

Our History

Founded last year, BlueStone initially explored decentralized finance (DeFi) but pivoted to NFT-based solutions due to regulatory challenges. We are already engaging with potential clients in the crypto ticketing sector and are poised to expand into the gaming industry.

Our Location

BlueStone is headquartered in Peterborough, Canada, with a market focus on North America, Australia, and Southeast Asia. Our team operates with a hybrid model, combining a centralized office in Peterborough with strategic remote teams, enhancing our local and international presence.
